Unfiltered — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 February 2026

Overview

Unfiltered is a personal mental health journaling app designed to help you reflect on your thoughts and emotions. Your privacy is extremely important to us — especially given the sensitive nature of the content you may share within the app. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data We Do Not Collect

Unfiltered does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information
  • We do not require you to create an account or log in
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use advertising or ad tracking
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not send any data to external servers or cloud services

Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create within Unfiltered — including journal entries, emotional check-ins, mood data, and app settings — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be permanently removed.

No Cloud Services

Unfiltered does not connect to any cloud services, APIs, or external servers. The app functions entirely offline. Your journal entries and emotional data remain completely private on your device.

Sensitive Data

We recognise that the thoughts, feelings, and reflections you record in Unfiltered may be deeply personal. We have designed the app with this in mind — no data ever leaves your device, and we have no ability to access, read, or analyse your entries.

Children's Privacy

Unfiltered does not collect any personal information from any users, including children. No account creation is required, and no data is transmitted from the device.

Third-Party Services

Unfiltered does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
